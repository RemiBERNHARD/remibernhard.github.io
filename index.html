About me

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate studying adversarial machine learning in a cooperation between CEA (French public reseach institute) and the SAS (Security of Architectures and Systems) research team of the engineering school "Ecole des Mines de Saint-Etienne".
My research work focuses on adversarial examples and is done under the supervision of Pierre-Alain Moellic and Jean-Max Dutertre.
Before that, I graduated from the engineering school ENSIIE (computer science, statistics and applied mathematics) and obtained a MSc degree in statistical learning from the Université Paris-Saclay.
